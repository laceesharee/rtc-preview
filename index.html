Terms of Service Agreement

By scheduling an appointment, paying a deposit, or receiving services from Round The Clock Notary, you agree to the following terms and conditions in full.

Service Description

Round The Clock Notary provides mobile notary and remote online notarization services in accordance with Texas law. The notary does not represent any party and does not participate in the legal content of documents.

Legal Advice Disclaimer

The notary public is not an attorney and does not provide legal advice. Consult a licensed attorney if legal guidance is needed.

Identification Requirements

All signers must present valid government-issued photo ID. The name on the ID must match the document being notarized.

Deposit & Appointment Policy

Deposits are non-refundable if cancellation is less than 24 hours, signer fails to appear, or appointment cannot be completed due to missing ID or incomplete documents.

Travel Fees

Travel fees apply once the notary begins travel and are non-refundable even if the notarization cannot be completed.

Limitation of Liability

Liability is limited to the lesser of total fees paid or the notary E&O insurance limits.

No Call / No Show

If the notary is not contacted within 15 minutes of the scheduled appointment time, the appointment is a no-show and the deposit is forfeited.

Governing Law

This agreement is governed by Texas law. Disputes shall be mediated in Hood County, Texas before any legal action.
